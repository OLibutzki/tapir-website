Editions

Users can request a personal license free of charge for testing non-commercial software. This license is not limited by the number of Page Objects or Test Cases.

For testing commercial software we offer a corresponding edition. As we expect your requirements to be very heterogeneous contact us in order to get an offer which fulfills all your needs.
